The Ultimate Protein Shake


The ultimate protein shake will NOT give you extremely bad poos, nor will it destroy your guts. Instead, it will leave you begging for more.

With 65g protein per serving you will feel stronger than ever.


Here is what you will need:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 scoops of mega buster protein
  • 400ml milk
  • 1 chunk of dairy milk chocolate

Now here is what you must do:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk
  2. Add the FIRST scoop of protein powder and continue to whisk
  3. Add all of the milk and continue to whisk
  4. Melt the chocolate and leave to cool
  5. Add the second scoop of protein powder and whisk
  6. Finally, add the melted chocolate, stir and enjoy!
